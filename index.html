Lusjes Art

Patern design and handdyed yarn

About us

Lusjes Art started out a few years ago as a hobby gone wild. I couldn't find the right elements in existing patterns, so I decided to design my own and share them with the world. I expanded with dying yarn and now all this is also available for everybody





Yarn

Hand dyed yarn, in any color you like. Spotted, striped, name it and I'll try to make it. I have a few in stock and they can be reproduced as well. Most skeins are enough for one shawl or a pair of socks, but custom orders can be made for larger skeins as well. Contact me for the details





Patterns

Patterns designed by myself. A few beautiful shawls, but also the popular hat that Rory wore in the serie "Gilmore Girls" is exactly reproduced and you can walk around like Rory yourself with matching mittens and a cowl. Look at my Ravelry page for all my patterns.
